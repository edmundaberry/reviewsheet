$\text{Var}(X)$ & $=$ & $\mathbb{E}[X^2] - \left(\mathbb{E}[X]\right)^2$  \\
\hline
$\text{Cov}(X, Y)$ & $=$ & $\mathbb{E}[X\cdot Y] - \mathbb{E}[X]\cdot\mathbb{E}[Y]$  \\
\hline
$\rho_{X, Y}$ & $=$ & $\frac{\text{Cov}(X, Y)}{\sigma_X\cdot\sigma_Y}$ \\
\hline
$\text{Var}(a A + b B + c)$ & $=$ & $a^2 \text{Var}(A) + b^2 \text{Var}(B) + 2\cdot a \cdot b \cdot \text{Cov}(A, B) $ \\
